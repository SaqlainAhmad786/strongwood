FAQ

Essential Information on Wooden Crates for Safe Shipping

  • What services do you offer?
    We manufacture custom wooden crates, export packaging, ISPM-15 heat-treated crates, and provide industrial and on-site packing solutions for shipments of all sizes.
    How do I get started with your services?
    Contact us with your cargo details such as size, weight, and destination. Our team will recommend the right crate design and provide a clear quotation.
    Do you provide export-compliant packaging?
    Yes, all our export crates are heat-treated and compliant with ISPM-15 standards, making them suitable for international shipments.
    What types of cargo can you pack?
    We pack machinery, industrial equipment, fragile goods, heavy cargo, and non-standard shipments requiring custom wooden packaging.
    How long does it take to manufacture a wooden crate?
    Production timelines depend on crate size and complexity. Standard crates are usually completed within 2–5 working days, while custom or heavy-duty crates may take slightly longer.
